# csm.stakeholder-map

Builds and updates stakeholder maps for accounts — relationship health, engagement gaps, sponsor risk, and coverage of economic buyer, champion, and influencer roles.

## Use it for

- Build stakeholder map for an account
- Gap analysis of missing stakeholder roles
- Sponsor risk assessment

## Don't use it for

- Account-level health review (use csm.health-score-review)
- Research on specific individuals (use csm.account-research --stakeholders)

## How to trigger it

Say something like:

- "stakeholder map"
- "who are our stakeholders at [account]"
- "champion risk"
- "sponsor risk"
- "executive sponsor"

## What you get

- Stakeholder map (structured markdown)
- Gap analysis
- Sponsor risk assessment

## Prerequisites

- csm CLAUDE.md
- Known contacts at the account

## Governance

- Relationship health ratings are [Moderate] confidence without engagement data
