# rev-ops.revenue-brief-generation

Produces weekly or monthly executive revenue narrative by coordinating outputs from all rev-ops agents. Five sections: forecast + pipeline, pipeline health, CS capacity status, NRR trajectory, top 3 risks with recommended owner and action. One decision required per brief. Delivered as DRAFT for RevOps lead review before distribution.

## Use it for

- Produce weekly executive revenue narrative from live connector data
- Synthesize cross-functional revenue picture into one coherent story
- Surface the single most important decision leadership needs to make

## Don't use it for

- Replacing pipeline-coverage-analysis, deal-health-scoring, or other source skills
- Final distribution without RevOps lead review

## How to trigger it

Say something like:

- "revenue brief"
- "weekly summary"
- "executive summary"
- "what happened this week"
- "run the revenue brief"

## What you get

- Five-section executive revenue brief (DRAFT)
- One named decision required this week

## Prerequisites

- All practice profile fields
- Live connector data from HubSpot, CS platform
- Source skill outputs (pipeline-coverage-analysis, deal-health-scoring, etc.)

## Governance

- G1 — Section 1 forecast qualification mandatory
- G2 — Section 3 CS capacity structural qualifier mandatory
- G6 — data-as-of on every section
- G7 — Section 5 risks each require escalation path and named owner
- Delivered as DRAFT — RevOps lead reviews before distribution
- Unavailable source sections labeled clearly; brief still produced with available sections
