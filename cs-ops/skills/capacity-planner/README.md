# cs-ops.capacity-planner

Assess CSM capacity and coverage across the current book of business. Models headcount demand, coverage ratios, and book-of-business distribution to surface under- or over-capacity signals at the team and segment level.

## Use it for

- Current capacity and coverage ratio audit
- Headcount demand modeling for growth scenarios
- Book redistribution planning after departures or segment changes
- Pre-QBR capacity review for CS leadership

## Don't use it for

- Hiring decisions or approval (produces recommendations only)
- Individual performance assessment
- Renewal or churn forecasting

## How to trigger it

Say something like:

- "capacity"
- "coverage ratio"
- "CSM headcount"
- "book of business distribution"
- "who should take the account"
- "can we absorb new logos"

## What you get

- Capacity and coverage ratio report
- Headcount demand model
- Redistribution recommendation

## Prerequisites

- cs-ops CLAUDE.md practice profile
- CSM roster with segment assignments and ARR

## Governance

- Recommendations only — no autonomous roster changes
- Headcount proposals require CS leadership approval
