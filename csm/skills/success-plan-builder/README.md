# csm.success-plan-builder

Builds and updates customer success plans — co-authored documents with measurable outcomes, milestones, and ownership. Aligned to the outcome catalog and customer lifecycle stage.

## Use it for

- New success plan from scratch
- Reset and rebuild an existing plan
- Quarterly success plan review

## Don't use it for

- Onboarding-stage success criteria (use onboarding.success-criteria)
- QBR construction (use csm.qbr-builder)

## How to trigger it

Say something like:

- "success plan"
- "build success plan"
- "update success plan"
- "customer success plan"

## What you get

- Success plan document (markdown)

## Prerequisites

- csm CLAUDE.md
- Outcome catalog path
- Customer goals and lifecycle stage

## Governance

- Success plan is co-authored — requires customer review before finalizing
